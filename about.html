About us

Flowershop is a Member of an International award winning network of skilled professional Florists which provide fabulous floral designs for all occasions.We buy Fresh flowers daily to ensure that you - our most valued customers and your clients, family or friends are completely satisfied when receiving our fresh long lasting floral arrangements.

We provides excellent customer service both on line through the internet and off line through our customer service centre. Our friendly fully trained staff can assist you in ordering your flowers and suggest suitable floral arrangements to meet your special requirements.

We also provide a wide range of great gifts including Hampers, Teddy's and Ticket packages. Call us on 1300 691 625. We make purchasing easy and safe by offering a secure on line booking service using the most advanced technology available including high security encryption coding on all transactions. Our Policy is to provide excellent service and fabulous flowers at all times to our valued customers. We are confident once you order with us you will return again and again.
